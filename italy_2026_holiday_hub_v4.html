What the family said they were most interested in.

The preference pattern from the family questionnaire points towards a relaxed but active week: lake time, boats, island hopping, swimming, paddleboard/kayak, Stresa shops/cafes/lakeside time, markets, walking/hiking, bike hire, food and dining, plus some proper adventure for those who want it.

Adventure appetite
